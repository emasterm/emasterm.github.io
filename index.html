About Me

Most graduates get caps and gowns. I have this blog to show for my final semester at Virginia Tech. As such, I welcome you to a page of bizarre musings courtesy of a 22-year-old 0L- I call myself a 0L because despite having already been admitted into a law school, I have no way of knowing if I'll ever actually physically attend my school of choice (so wear your masks, I guess!). I'd consider myself a dog person with too many majors. My frontyard looks like a junkyard because we have severeal broken-down cars out front. I'm an essential worker because I'm a delivery driver. We're all stuck inside, so you might as well peruse this site. Enjoy!
